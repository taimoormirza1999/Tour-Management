Description

Leisure Inn has a small café, garden and a terrace completes it for a quiet and a peaceful getaway both for work and leisure. Double Bedroom with a fully-equipped separate kitchen, bathrooms and balconies. All the rooms of Leisure Inn have an attached bathroom and a separate study. Fire extinguishers, earthquake alarm and 24-Hours surveillance cameras are installed for added security.
